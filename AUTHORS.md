## Contributors

Thank you to everyone who has contributed to this project.

### How to Add Yourself as a Contributor

When you make a contribution to this project (code, documentation, or other), feel free to add yourself to this list in alphabetical order by following this format:

- Your Name <your.email@example.com> (contributor)

Example:
- John Doe <john.doe@example.com> (contributor - GPU performance improvements)

Please include a brief description of your contributions in parentheses after "(contributor)".
